About Track Machines in Indian Railways

Track machines are essential for maintaining and improving the vast railway network of Indian Railways. These machines are used for track laying, track renewal, track maintenance, and other vital functions to ensure the safe and efficient operation of trains across the country.

Track Machines

Purpose of the System

The Track Machine Management System is designed to monitor and manage the performance, maintenance, and failures of track machines. This system aims to enhance the efficiency, reliability, and safety of railway operations.
